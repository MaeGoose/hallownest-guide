Charms in Hollow Knight are one of the main ways one can affect their gameplay directly. From faster nail swings, dashes, faster heals, defensive charms and many more; one can get easily overwhelmed with the amount of charms there are in the game. In total there are 40 charms the player can have at one time. Listed below are some of the most used notches in the game and a video essay by Technovoid regarding the analysis and ranking of every charm in the game

Most Commonly Used Charms

Wayward Compass
Always know where you are.

Shaman Stone
Stronger spells for magic-focused builds.

Quick Slash
Unleash rapid nail strikes.

Long Nail
Extend your reach in battle.
